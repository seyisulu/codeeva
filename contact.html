Codeeva is an initiative developed to bolster female engagement in the Nigerian (and by extension, African) IT industry through training and mentorships. We aim to sustain a community of ladies proficient in bleeding edge development technologies, tools and industry best practices. The scope of the training will include HTML5, CSS3, isomorphic JavaScript development with MeteorJS, NoSQL database modeling using MongoDB, cloud deployments (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform & Heroku) and distributed version control systems.
